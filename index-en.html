The Triple Path of the Solution

The Poincaré Conjecture reveals a deep correlation among three knowledge systems: automatic quantum structure, the scientific rigor of Domus Horizon through discrete networks, and the traditional analytical proof by Perelman. Although each path starts from a different paradigm, all three converge towards the same spherical topology, confirming that the solution does not depend on a single method, but on the synchrony between geometry, computation and pure mathematical thought.
