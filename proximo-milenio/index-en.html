Synopsis of the Mathematical Development

This technical portal hosts rigorous data analysis based on mega-scale simulations (run on a distributed-node Debian Linux architecture). Through a Deterministic Geometric Oracle (DGO) and the analytical formalization of the asymmetric bell using modified Runge functions, a new paradigm is established in the resolution and practical limits of polynomial computation over NP-hard graphs.
